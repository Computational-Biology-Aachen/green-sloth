The Gu2023 model is a steady-state model of the photochemical reactions of photosynthesis that builds upon previous modeling efforts, most of which consist of ordinary differential equations. It provides a simplification of the state equations, to provide redox relationships between the linear electrton transport ($J_{PSII}$), the fraction of open PSII reaction centres ($q$), the fraction of the plastoquinone pool that is oxidized ($h_{PQ}$), and the fraction of the cytochrome b6f complex that is available for linear electron transfer ($h_{cyt}$). Additionally, it includes the thylakoid ultrastructural dynamics, which have previously been ignored in many non-steady-state models.
